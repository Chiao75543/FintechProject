About Stonk

This system is a stock prediction system designed for novice stock investors and investors who do not have time to observe the stock market in real time. The system will use machine learning technology to provide prediction of stock trading timing and investment strategy reference to help users make more informed investment decisions and validate their investment strategies.



What can you do with Stonk ?

Simply enter the stock code to find out which day the stock will rise in the next 30 days！
